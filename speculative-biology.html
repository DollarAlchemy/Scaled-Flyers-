Biological Inspirations for Fire-Breathing

  • Flint Ingestion: Birds ingest grit for digestion, which could evolve into spark production.
  • Oil-Secreting Glands: Similar to seabirds’ waterproofing oils, but flammable.
  • Bombardier Beetle Chemistry: Explosive sprays demonstrate how controlled chemical reactions can evolve.
  • Electric Eels: Precision in energy discharge offers parallels to ignition mechanisms.
