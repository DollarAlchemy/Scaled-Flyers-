Overview

Dragons have long been symbols of power and mystery. Could fire-breathing traits plausibly evolve in real organisms? This section introduces the scope of this speculative study, which combines natural adaptations and evolutionary principles.

Objectives

  • To explore plausible evolutionary pathways for fire-breathing traits.
  • To critique anthropocentric constructs of intelligence using ecological and philosophical insights.
